Crisis Center

Sexual assaults should be reported to the police department that has jurisdiction (i.e., the police department in the town/city where the assault took place). Learn more... You may go directly to your local hospital and ask to speak with an officer. If you do not wish to go to the hospital, an officer can meet with you in a safe place to take a brief, initial report. The officer may also encourage you to seek medical attention to ensure that you receive proper care and follow-up. It is your right to have a confidential advocate from your local crisis center accompany you to either the local police department or hospital.
